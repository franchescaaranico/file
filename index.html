Vulnerability is terrifying. To reveal your true self is to place a fragile piece of your soul into someone else's hands, trusting that they will hold it gently. Yet there is always the possibility that they may distort it, reject it, or simply walk away. Often, it is not the wounds people leave behind that haunt us the longest, but the emptiness that follows as they leave. And still, we do it again and again, because even pain feels safer than uncertainty.
