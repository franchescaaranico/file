ABOUT

Though who I am is of no importance, my identity is as fluid as my beliefs. Life might have been magical and rosy if not for the skeptical worm in my head that constantly counterargues every belief I hold. I seem to self-actualize only by destroying the “I am” a hundred times over.

If you’ve somehow found your way here expecting wisdom or amusement, your expectations are likely misplaced. At best, I’m a seemingly sane person writing about a life spent stumbling through varying degrees of success and shame. At worst, I’m the woman you passed on the street, the one your parent pointed to while saying, Never be like that woman.

About the site:
It exists as a personal record of my questionable philosophies, passing curiosities, and the generally unremarkable banalities of ordinary life. Also, because I am rarely on social media. It may come off as self-masturbatory because, well, in many ways, it is.

At this point, I exist on the internet only because this site does. This is an imprint of my shameless digital footprints and personal history, things that would, by most reasonable standards, be better left buried.

I write sincerely and make things for myself first because she’s the only audience I can reliably disappoint. Everyone else is welcome to look around, but understand that you are, at best, a confused guest wandering through someone else’s unfinished archive.
